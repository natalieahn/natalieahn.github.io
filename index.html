Natalie Ahn

Data scientist/computational social scientist, with prior research focused on political institutions and the allocation of power in developing governments, using natural language processing, machine learning, and other computational methods for information extraction, data processing and statistical analysis. Currently a data scientist at Hoodline/Pixel Labs, working on the development of their automation pipeline for data-driven local news. Previously worked in journalism and foreign policy, focusing on U.S. defense policy, political-military coordination and post-conflict reconstruction in Washington, DC and Iraq.
